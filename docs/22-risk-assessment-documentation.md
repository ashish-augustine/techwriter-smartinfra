# Risk Assessment Documentation

Objectives

- Provide standards for documenting risk assessments, risk registers and mitigation plans.

Scope

- Asset, project and operational risk assessments.

Stakeholders

- Risk Manager, Safety Lead, Asset Manager.

Deliverables

- Risk templates, risk register, scoring matrices.

Processes

- Identify → assess → mitigate → monitor.

Governance Mechanisms

- Risk committee, periodic review schedule.

Templates

- Risk assessment form, risk register CSV/YAML.

Metrics

- Number of high risks mitigated, residual risk trends.

Risks

- Underreporting, inconsistent scoring.

Mitigation Strategies

- Standard scoring guides, training for assessors.

Best Practices

- Link risks to documentation action items and owners.
