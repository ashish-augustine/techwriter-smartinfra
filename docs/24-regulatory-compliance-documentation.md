# Regulatory Compliance Documentation

Objectives

- Document compliance activities, evidence and traceability for regulatory requirements including the NZ Building Code.

Scope

- Regulatory submissions, compliance trace matrices, audit evidence.

Stakeholders

- Legal/Compliance, Asset Manager, Project Sponsor.

Deliverables

- Compliance checklists, submission packages, traceability matrices.

Processes

- Map requirements to controls, collect evidence, maintain a compliance ledger.

Governance Mechanisms

- Compliance owner, audit schedule, evidence retention policies.

Templates

- Compliance trace matrix, evidence index.

Metrics

- Audit findings, time to resolve non-conformances.

Risks

- Missing evidence, changing regulations.

Mitigation Strategies

- Continuous monitoring and periodic regulatory reviews.

Best Practices

- Keep traceability from requirement → control → evidence explicit and versioned.
