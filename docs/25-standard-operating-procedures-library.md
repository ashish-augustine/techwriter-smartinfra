# Standard Operating Procedures Library

Objectives

- Maintain a curated library of SOPs for operations, maintenance, and emergency activities.

Scope

- SOPs for routine and safety-critical tasks across assets.

Stakeholders

- Operations, Maintenance, Safety, Training.

Deliverables

- SOP templates, indexed SOP library with metadata and owners.

Processes

- SOP authoring → SME review → trial → publish → periodic review.

Governance Mechanisms

- Owner sign-off and review cadence; version history retained in Git.

Templates

- SOP template with Purpose, Scope, Preconditions, Steps, Postconditions, Safety.

Metrics

- SOP coverage of critical tasks, review compliance.

Risks

- Outdated procedures causing safety issues.

Mitigation Strategies

- Mandatory review schedules and incident-driven updates.

Best Practices

- Keep SOPs step-by-step, include acceptance criteria and required PPE.
