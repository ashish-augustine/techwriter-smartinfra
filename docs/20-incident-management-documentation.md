# Incident Management Documentation

Objectives

- Establish standard incident workflows, priority levels, and reporting templates.

Scope

- Operational incidents, service outages, safety incidents.

Stakeholders

- Incident Manager, Support Teams, Communications.

Deliverables

- Incident classification scheme, ticketing templates, post-incident review template.

Processes

- Incident intake → triage → resolution → RCA and closure.

Governance Mechanisms

- Incident review boards, escalation thresholds.

Templates

- Incident ticket template, RCA template.

Metrics

- MTTR, number of repeated incidents, time to escalate.

Risks

- Misclassification, delayed escalation.

Mitigation Strategies

- Clear classification matrix and escalation SLAs.

Best Practices

- Automate ticket enrichment (attach logs, photos) and keep timelines concise.
