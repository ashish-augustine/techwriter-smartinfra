# Emergency Response Documentation

Objectives

- Document incident response procedures, roles, and escalation paths for major infrastructure incidents.

Scope

- Major incidents affecting safety, service continuity, or environment.

Stakeholders

- Emergency Manager, Operations, Communications, Safety Lead.

Deliverables

- Emergency response plan, contact lists, escalation paths, public communications templates.

Processes

- Incident detection → initial response → escalation → post-incident review.

Governance Mechanisms

- Emergency committee sign-off, simulation schedule.

Templates

- Incident report, public notification templates, checklists.

Metrics

- Incident detection-to-response time, resolution time.

Risks

- Poor coordination, outdated contact info.

Mitigation Strategies

- Regular drills, maintain contact databases and redundant communications.

Best Practices

- Clear roles and runbooks; integrate with local emergency services.
