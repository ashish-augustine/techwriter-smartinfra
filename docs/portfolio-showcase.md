# Portfolio Showcase

This portfolio demonstrates enterprise-level documentation leadership for smart infrastructure, transportation, utilities, and civil engineering programs.

## Why this portfolio matters

As a Senior Technical Writer, I build documentation programs that align with governance, regulatory compliance, stakeholder workflows, and digital transformation goals. This portfolio is designed to show:

- strategic documentation program design
- governance and content ownership models
- knowledge management and information architecture
- templates, dashboards, and execution roadmaps
- measurable outcomes and portfolio leadership

## Senior-level strengths

- Program leadership: define documentation strategy, governance, roadmap, and executive reporting.
- Cross-functional engagement: coordinate engineers, subject matter experts, safety teams, and business stakeholders.
- Standards and quality: deliver consistent templates, style guidance, review frameworks, and audit-ready documentation.
- Metrics-driven delivery: use dashboards, KPIs, and risk registers to measure documentation health and adoption.

## Selected case studies

### Governance dashboard and executive reporting

- Designed the governance dashboard structure, data definitions, and stakeholder cadence for the documentation program.
- Aligned KPI reporting with risk registers, content health metrics, and compliance requirements.
- Enabled senior leadership to make data-driven decisions with monthly executive reports and weekly operational snapshots.

### Documentation program blueprint

- Built a complete enterprise documentation architecture across 50 portfolio sections.
- Incorporated taxonomy design, metadata standards, content lifecycle, and knowledge base strategy.
- Created templates and review frameworks that reduce duplication and accelerate onboarding.

### Knowledge management and search strategy

- Developed a search strategy that supports asset classification, stakeholder role-based access, and reuse of documentation.
- Established a knowledge management program that improves content discoverability for maintenance, operations, and emergency response teams.
