# Bridge Maintenance Documentation

Short one-page overview (requested). This page outlines documentation required for bridge maintenance activities.

Objectives

- Provide maintenance teams with clear procedures, inspection checklists, and asset records.

Scope

- Routine inspections, preventive maintenance schedules, condition assessments, and handover records.

Stakeholders

- Asset Manager, Maintenance Team, Safety Lead.

Deliverables

- Inspection checklist template, maintenance schedule, inspection reports, historical condition log.

Processes

- Inspections recorded in CMMS; official reports generated as PDFs and archived; urgent repairs trigger incident workflows.

Governance Mechanisms

- Assigned content owner (Asset Manager); quarterly review cycles; RACI for sign-offs.

Templates

- Bridge Inspection Checklist, Maintenance Job Template, Condition Assessment Form.

Metrics

- Inspection completion rate, average time to close maintenance tickets, condition trend metrics.

Risks

- Missed inspections, incomplete records, safety non-compliance.

Mitigation Strategies

- Automated CMMS reminders, mandatory fields on inspection forms, periodic audits.

Best Practices

- Use photographic evidence; link inspection records to GIS coordinates and asset IDs.
