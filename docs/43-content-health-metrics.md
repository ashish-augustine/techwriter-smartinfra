# Content Health Metrics

Objectives

- Define metrics that indicate the health of content across the documentation program.

Scope

- Freshness, ownership, searchability, usage and quality.

Stakeholders

- Content Owners, KM Lead, Analytics.

Deliverables

- Health scorecard, reporting schedule.

Processes

- Automated checks, periodic manual audits, remediation sprints.

Governance Mechanisms

- Health dashboard and owner accountability.

Templates

- Health report template.

Metrics

- Age of content, last review date, metadata completeness, error reports.

Risks

- High percentage of stale content.

Mitigation Strategies

- Prioritise high-impact content for remediation.

Best Practices

- Automate detection of stale or broken content and notify owners.
