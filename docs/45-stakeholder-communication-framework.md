# Stakeholder Communication Framework

Objectives

- Define communication cadences, audiences and channels for documentation changes and governance updates.

Scope

- Notifications for owners, executive summaries for sponsors, operational change notices.

Stakeholders

- Communications Lead, Content Owners, Sponsors.

Deliverables

- Communication plan, templates for updates, escalation messages.

Processes

- Scheduled updates, ad-hoc incident notifications, stakeholder sign-off on major changes.

Governance Mechanisms

- Communication owner and channel approvals.

Templates

- Email templates, stakeholder briefing pack.

Metrics

- Open rate on update emails, time to inform stakeholders.

Risks

- Information overload or missed recipients.

Mitigation Strategies

- Targeted audiences and concise summaries; maintain updated distribution lists.

Best Practices

- Use dashboards for routine updates and reserve email for critical escalations.
