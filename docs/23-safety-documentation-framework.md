# Safety Documentation Framework

Objectives

- Provide a structured approach to safety documentation, safety cases, checklists, and compliance evidence.

Scope

- Safety plans, permits, safety cases for high-risk assets and activities.

Stakeholders

- Safety Lead, Contractors, Asset Manager.

Deliverables

- Safety case templates, permit templates, assurance records.

Processes

- Hazard identification, control measures, verification and sign-off.

Governance Mechanisms

- Safety board approvals, documented evidence trails.

Templates

- Safety case template, checklist for high-risk activities.

Metrics

- Safety audit pass rates, number of hazards mitigated.

Risks

- Incomplete evidence, non-compliance with regulations.

Mitigation Strategies

- Standard evidence collection procedures and audit trails.

Best Practices

- Keep safety documentation concise, evidence-based, and versioned.
