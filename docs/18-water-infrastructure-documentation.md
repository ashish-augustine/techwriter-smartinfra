# Water Infrastructure Documentation

Objectives

- Document water networks, treatment processes, and asset management for reservoirs, pipelines, and treatment plants.

Scope

- Treatment plant manuals, pipeline inspection and rehabilitation records, emergency spill response.

Stakeholders

- Water Services Manager, Asset Manager, Environmental Compliance.

Deliverables

- Treatment SOPs, pipeline inspection reports, environmental compliance logs.

Processes

- Routine sampling and logging, planned maintenance, incident reporting.

Governance Mechanisms

- Regulatory compliance checks, environmental monitoring schedules.

Templates

- Sampling log template, rehabilitation plan.

Metrics

- Compliance pass rates, leak detection response time.

Risks

- Contamination events, supply disruptions.

Mitigation Strategies

- Redundant supply planning, rapid isolation procedures.

Best Practices

- Keep process diagrams updated and use time-stamped sampling data.
