# Documentation Vision and Strategy

Objectives

- Define the long-term documentation vision aligned to enterprise goals: safety, reliability, operational efficiency, and knowledge continuity.

Scope

- Enterprise-wide documentation strategy covering policy, operations, engineering, and training.

Stakeholders

- Senior Technical Writer (owner), Engineering Manager, Asset Manager, Knowledge Manager.

Deliverables

- Strategy brief, roadmap, measurable KPIs, governance model.

Processes

- Annual strategy review, quarterly roadmaps, stakeholder planning workshops.

Governance Mechanisms

- Steering committee, content advisory board, documented KPIs and cadence.

Templates

- Strategy one-pager, roadmap template, stakeholder engagement plan.

Metrics

- Strategy adoption rate, documentation ROI indicators, stakeholder satisfaction.

Risks

- Strategy misalignment with operations, insufficient stakeholder buy-in.

Mitigation Strategies

- Early stakeholder workshops, pilot projects, measurable milestones.

Best Practices

- Keep vision concise, measurable, and connected to operational KPIs.
