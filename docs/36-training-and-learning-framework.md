# Training and Learning Framework

Objectives

- Define training pathways, learning materials, and onboarding for documentation consumers and contributors.

Scope

- Induction, role-based training, certification tracks.

Stakeholders

- Training Lead, HR, Content Owners.

Deliverables

- Course outlines, learning modules, assessments.

Processes

- Create → deliver → assess → certify.

Governance Mechanisms

- Training schedule and certification authority.

Templates

- Course syllabus, slide templates, assessment rubric.

Metrics

- Training completion rate, assessment pass rate.

Risks

- Low uptake of training programs.

Mitigation Strategies

- Integrate training into onboarding and performance goals.

Best Practices

- Blend practical exercises, checklists, and assessments.
