# Maintenance Manual

Objectives

- Central reference for maintenance policies, procedures, and asset care instructions.

Scope

- Preventive, corrective, and predictive maintenance procedures.

Stakeholders

- Maintenance Manager, Technicians, Asset Manager.

Deliverables

- Maintenance manual, spare parts lists, inspection protocols.

Processes

- Scheduling, execution, and recording of maintenance tasks in CMMS.

Governance Mechanisms

- Maintenance plan approval, spare parts management policy.

Templates

- Maintenance task template, spare parts register.

Metrics

- Mean time between failures (MTBF), completion rates.

Risks

- Parts obsolescence, incomplete documentation.

Mitigation Strategies

- Parts lifecycle monitoring and documentation audits.

Best Practices

- Include diagrams and torque/spec values for mechanical tasks.
