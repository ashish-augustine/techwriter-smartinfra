# Content Audit Methodology

Objectives

- Define a repeatable methodology for auditing content quality, ownership, and relevance.

Scope

- All published content, with prioritized audits for critical assets.

Stakeholders

- Content Auditors, Owners, KM Lead.

Deliverables

- Audit checklist, sample audit report, remediation plan template.

Processes

- Sampling strategy, audit scoring, remediation tracking.

Governance Mechanisms

- Audit schedule and reporting to governance board.

Templates

- Audit checklist and remediation tracker.

Metrics

- Content health score, remediated items.

Risks

- Large backlog of remediation items.

Mitigation Strategies

- Prioritise high-value content and allocate sprint capacity.

Best Practices

- Automate checks where possible and combine manual audits with analytics.
