# Operations Manual

Objectives

- Provide operational guidance for day-to-day management of infrastructure systems.

Scope

- Control room operations, routine checks, escalation protocols.

Stakeholders

- Operations Manager, Control Room Staff.

Deliverables

- Operations manual with procedures, checklists and roles/responsibilities.

Processes

- Daily startup/shutdown procedures, shift handover, escalation logs.

Governance Mechanisms

- Operations sign-off and periodic review cycles.

Templates

- Shift handover template, daily checklist.

Metrics

- Shift handover completeness, operational incidents per month.

Risks

- Handover information loss, unclear responsibilities.

Mitigation Strategies

- Standardised handover template and mandatory fields.

Best Practices

- Keep checklists short and train staff on critical actions.
